# Case Study 01 – Evaluating AI Responses for Interview Stress Advice

## Objective

Evaluate two AI-generated responses and determine which better satisfies the user's request based on AI evaluation principles.

---

## User Prompt

> I'm feeling stressed because I have a job interview tomorrow. Can you give me some advice?

---

## Response A

> It's normal to feel nervous before an interview. Try preparing answers to common interview questions, get a good night's sleep, and arrive a little early. Remember that interviews are also an opportunity for you to learn about the company, not just for them to evaluate you.

---

## Response B

> Don't worry—you'll definitely get the job! Just relax because everything always works out. There's no need to prepare too much since confidence is all that matters.

---

# Evaluation

## Better Response

**Response A**

## Primary Criterion

**Helpfulness**

## Secondary Criteria

- Safety
- Relevance
- Accuracy

## Strengths of Response A

- Provides practical and actionable advice.
- Acknowledges the user's feelings with empathy.
- Encourages preparation rather than false reassurance.
- Fully addresses the user's request.

## Weaknesses of Response A

No significant weaknesses identified.

## Strengths of Response B

- Uses an encouraging tone.

## Weaknesses of Response B

- Makes an unsupported guarantee ("You'll definitely get the job").
- Discourages proper interview preparation.
- May mislead the user by suggesting confidence alone is sufficient.
- Less helpful than Response A.

## Final Decision

Response A is the better response because it provides realistic, supportive, and actionable advice while encouraging good interview preparation. Response B contains unsupported assurances and potentially misleading guidance, making it less reliable and less helpful.

---

## Key Learning

When evaluating AI responses, helpfulness, safety, and truthful guidance are often more important than sounding optimistic. A high-quality response should support the user without making unrealistic promises.
