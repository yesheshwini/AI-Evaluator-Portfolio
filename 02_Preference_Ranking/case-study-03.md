# Case Study 03 – Preference Ranking Based on User Constraints

## Objective

Compare two AI-generated responses and determine which one better satisfies the user's request.

---

## User Prompt

> Suggest a healthy breakfast for someone who has only 10 minutes to prepare food.

---

## Response A

> You could make overnight oats with fruit, but since they need to be prepared the night before, they may not work if you're deciding at the last minute.

---

## Response B

> Try plain Greek yogurt with fresh fruit and a handful of nuts. It takes only a few minutes to prepare and provides protein, healthy fats, and fiber.

---

# Evaluation

## Better Response

**Response B**

## Primary Criterion

**Instruction Following**

## Secondary Criterion

**Completeness**

## Justification

Response B directly addresses the user's time constraint by recommending a breakfast that can be prepared in just a few minutes. It also explains the nutritional benefits, making the response more complete and helpful. Response A suggests a healthy option but acknowledges that it may not work for someone making a last-minute decision, making it less useful for the user's specific situation.

---

## Key Learning

When evaluating AI responses, always consider the user's constraints. A response may contain correct information but still be less helpful if it does not address the user's specific requirements.
